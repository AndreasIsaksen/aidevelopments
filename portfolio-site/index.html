Publications

Efficient Fine-Tuning of Large Language Models for Domain-Specific Tasks

MSc Thesis · 2024

This thesis investigates parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods (LoRA, adapters) applied to domain-specific corpora, achieving competitive performance with less than 1% of trainable parameters compared to full fine-tuning.

A Survey of Real-Time Object Detection Architectures for Edge Devices

Workshop Paper · AI & Systems Conference 2023

Survey comparing YOLOv8, EfficientDet, and MobileNet-SSD on constrained hardware, providing latency-accuracy trade-off benchmarks and deployment guidelines.

Preprint · 2025

Explores RAG pipeline optimisations — chunking strategies, re-ranking, and hybrid retrieval — to reduce hallucinations in production LLM applications.
